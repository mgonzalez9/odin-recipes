Quesadillas

Description

This recipe is for quesadillas. Simple and quick snack to get you through the day!

Ingredients

  • Your choice of flour or corn tortillas.
  • Monterey Jack cheese (or any melty cheese).
  • (optional) Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat up pan with a teaspoon of cooking oil.
  2. Place shredded cheese onto on side of the tortilla.
  3. Fold the tortilla in half and place onto hot pan.
  4. Flip to cook other side and remove once cheese is fully melted. Enjoy!
