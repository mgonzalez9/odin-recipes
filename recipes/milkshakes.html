Strawberry Milkshakes

Description

This recipe is for strawberry milkshakes. This is a fruity twist on a vanilla milkshake.

Ingredients

  • Strawberry icecream
  • Whole milk
  • (optional)Fresh strawberries

Steps

  1. Scoop 3 cups of your favorite strawberry ice cream into a blender.
  2. Pour in 5 cups of whole milk into the blender.
  3. Blend until well combined.
  4. Pour into your favorite cup and enjoy!
  5. (optional) top with slices of fresh strawberry.
