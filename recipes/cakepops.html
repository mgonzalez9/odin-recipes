Cakepops

Description

This recipe is for cakepops. A fun way to endulge in a cake in one to two bites

Ingredients

  • cake mix
  • 2 eggs
  • milk
  • icing
  • candy melts
  • coconut oil
  • butter

Steps

  1. Mix cake mix, eggs, and milk in a bowl.
  2. Place mix into a pan and bake in oven until ready.
  3. Let cake cool down before crumbling finely.
  4. mix icing and cake crumbs until fully combined.
  5. Roll into 1 inch balls and set on parchement paper.
  6. Melt candy melts with some coconut oil.
  7. Dip tip of stick into candy melt and insert into ball.
  8. Fridge for 2 hours.
  9. Dip ball into candy melt. Fridge to harden. Enjoy!
